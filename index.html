Danielle "Dee" Morvant

My name is Danielle Morvant (friends and family call me Dee) and I am a New Iberia Louisiana native. I love spending time with family and friends and consider myself a homebody. I am expecting my firstborn with a man I thank God for everyday. We have a 10 year old chihuahua, a 2 year old grey tabby cat, and multiple nano fish aquariums complete with aqua scapes. My peace and joy comes with tending to my plants and whatever arts and crafts I currently have going on. I love being outside, tending to landscape details and looking after my plants. One of my favorite parts of living in South Louisiana is sitting and enjoying the sound of rain. Campfires and crawfish boils are also on the top of my list as well.
