Danielle "Dee" Morvant

My name is Danielle Morvant (friends and family call me Dee) and I am a New Iberia Louisiana native. I am expecting my firstborn at age 39, I do not have any other children since my only other pregnancy resulted in a miscarriage. I have lots of nieces and nephews, whom call me 'Dee Dee.' I have always been very proud as an aunt of the things they accomplished and the young adults they've become. I have 3 siblings...all being brothers. I love all things outdoors...four wheelers, planting things, and especially love our rainy Louisiana Nights. I also love to cook as much as I love food. My oldest brother inspired me at a young age to draw and I love it still today.
